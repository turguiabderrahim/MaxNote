Martian Doge, abbreviation: $MAD, is a decentralized modal coin based on the BNB Chain, launched by the Martian Doge Foundation, a cryptocurrency powered by the Martian Doge community.
It is inspired by Musk's Mars mania: the future economic model of Mars will be based on cryptocurrency. In tribute to Musk and DogeCoin, Martian doge was created to be the necessary economic tool for future human landings on Mars.
MARTIAN DOGE is a new meta-universe crypto-digital ecology, an update and iteration of the traditional blockchain ecology, issuing new digital Token, providing the core foundation for future Martian socio-economic tools and opening up more new application scenarios.
The MARTIAN DOGE community is reforming the traditional financial sector and aims to reform the entire blockchain market. The new eponymous digital token MARTIAN DOGE issued by the community deepens the depth of the DeFi field and builds a new consensus concept and pass economic model of the metaverse game ecology.
MARTIAN DOGE leverages a highly autonomous community with its own liquid currency, allowing users to build new content, socialization, order and economy in a world of their own.
MARTIAN DOGE establishes a new game play and LP pledge mining operation model, aiming to build DeFi+Web3.0+GAMEFI+NFT decentralized metaverse ecosystem.
MARTIAN DOGE uses the high-end technology of multi-chain + cross-chain to provide a more secure and efficient network for NFT and metaverse commodity trading market.
MARTIAN DOGE's Web3.0 system provides user-friendly trading scenarios for users to create, buy, sell, or exchange NFT and metaverse commodities easily, quickly, and securely, and MARTIAN DOGE supports NFT in a series of smart contract networks, including Coinsafe, Opensea, etc.,
bringing unlimited possibilities for NFT trading and derivation. In the MARTIAN DOGE platform, NFT-based assets can be interfaced into the virtual metaverse world to create a dream linkage with the metaverse.
For example, pledging NFT to generate a specific series of utility scenarios for the metaverse, pledging proceeds, etc., provides digital currency value transfer to the virtual world. In the MARTIAN DOGE metaverse ecology, the vehicle for transferring value is blockchain technology.
Through decentralized virtual asset equity records and virtual identity, blockchain technology provides a decentralized clearing and settlement platform, thus constructing a perfect value transfer mechanism
