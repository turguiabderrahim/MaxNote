Martian Doge NFT

In-game assets represented by NFT are expected to be bought, sold and traded in large numbers on the sidechain of the MARTIAN DOGE ecosystem in the future.
Developers can also save game state on the sidechain if they wish.
The full name of NFT is non-fungible token - non-homogenized token, NFT is derived from the economic concept of "irreplaceable token", which also gives a direct understanding of its "irreplaceable " nature. We can think of it simply as giving a digital work of art a unique ID in the world, and the person who has that ID is the owner of the work. Although it is very easy to copy and paste things that exist on the Internet, only those who have this ID mark are the genuine ones.
The NFT domain of MARTIAN DOGE ecology will be divided into three segments, excluding the chain tour domain, the other two domains are the artwork domain and the synthetic asset domain.
  • Art field: More and more art creators and ordinary consumers are entering the NFT field. Digital artwork is just one of the areas where NFT is being applied, and the design thinking behind it may be changing the relationship between the virtual and real worlds. Owning an NFT artwork is the equivalent of having on-chain credentials for art assets.
Crypto art projects do not necessarily require a so-called business model, and the standards of various artworks are tied to the preferences of their audiences. But NFT itself carries its own cryptocurrency properties, and the upcoming NFT platform created by MARTIAN DOGE Eco, which brings together thousands of artists and hundreds of thousands of works worldwide, has set a set of standards for project design as well as distribution for NFT crypto art projects. The NFT platform will be a standard for project design and distribution.
  • NFT Asset Synthesis: MARTIAN DOGE Eco will create a new NFT synthesis protocol, users can synthesize assets and mint new coins, and the synthesized new Token can be put online to generate interest, which is also a credential for users to deposit assets in MARTIAN DOGE Eco.
In the lending market, locked funds can only be used after they are unlocked, and when they are withdrawn, the amount of funds credited to the lending pool will be reduced as well. With the introduction of the new synthetic Token, this side effect can be avoided, as the funds deposited on the MARTIAN DOGE ecosystem will be available on the open market in full. The new synthetic Token will not affect the funds locked in the platform and therefore will not reduce the amount of funds available to the borrower.
