Martian Doge gamefi

GAMEFI developed by MARTIAN DOGE is a NFT role card game, users can buy MARTIAN DOGE NFT through $MAD to enter the game, but also through contributions to the community to get rewards airdrop NFT
​
MAD WARRIOR In the future development of MAD GAMEFI has a unique skill mark, consume a certain number of "$MAD" to get a weapon
MAD KILLER In GAMEFI in the "killer role" can hide ambush so that others do not know your true identity, and thus end the life of the opponent's NFT character. "It's a fun existence"
MAD KING As the king of the game, he is able to manipulate many character cards and has the ability to swap character cards at once. This allows him to obtain quality minions
MAD DOCTOR He has the ability to revive any character deck by saving it once
MAD SOLDIER The most common character, but it is the only one that can buy items
